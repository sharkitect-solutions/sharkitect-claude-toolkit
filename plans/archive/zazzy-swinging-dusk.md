# Sharkitect Digital Landing Page — Section-by-Section Optimization Plan

## Context
Chris wants to restructure and optimize the sharkitectdigital.com landing page. This is intentionally a landing page (not a full website) — a full site comes later. Goals:
- Add images and visual variety to break up the text wall
- Broaden language from HVAC/plumbing-specific to all home service businesses
- Optimize copy, layout, and conversion flow section by section
- Fix SEO/technical issues identified in the audit
- Reduce form friction

**Key constraints:** HubSpot CMS. Landing page (not full site). No nav menu needed yet. Chris will apply changes in HubSpot editor based on this guide.

---

## Global Changes (Apply Across All Sections)

### Visual System
- **Alternate section backgrounds:** Dark navy (current) → light/white → accent blue → dark navy. This creates visual rhythm and breaks the wall effect.
- **Replace all cyan text-link CTAs with actual buttons.** Use the style from the bottom CTA (rounded pill, gradient fill, arrow icon). Every CTA should look like a clickable button, not a heading.
- **Typography:** Current is fine (clean, readable). Just add more breathing room between sections.
- **Color palette:** Keep dark navy (#0a1628-ish) + cyan (#00e5ff-ish) + white. Add a light gray (#f5f7fa) for alternating sections and a warmer accent for trust/proof sections.

### Language Broadening
**Replace** all instances of "HVAC, plumbing, and home service" with broader framing:
- Primary: "home service businesses" or "home service companies"
- When listing examples: "HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, flooring, landscaping, and other home service trades"
- Never lead with HVAC/plumbing alone — lead with "home service" and use trades as examples

### Form Simplification
**Cut the /get-started form to 5 fields:**
1. Full name*
2. Email*
3. Phone*
4. Company name*
5. What's your biggest operational challenge? (optional textarea — keep this, it's gold for pre-call prep)

**Remove:** Last name (merged), Role, Service Industry, State/Region, "Which is costing you most?" dropdown, repetitive task textarea. These can be gathered during the diagnostic call itself.

**Change submit button** from "Get Started" → "Book My Free Diagnostic"
