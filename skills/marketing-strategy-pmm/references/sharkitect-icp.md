# Sharkitect Digital Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary ICP: The Overwhelmed Operator

### Demographics
- **Business type**: Service-based SMB (trades, professional services, agencies)
- **Revenue**: $500K-$5M annual
- **Team size**: 5-50 employees
- **Location**: US-based (remote service delivery)
- **Decision-maker**: Owner/CEO or Operations Manager

### Psychographics
- Good at their craft, drowning in operations
- Know they need systems but don't know where to start
- Have tried 2-3 tools (Zapier, HubSpot free, etc.) with limited success
- Spending 20+ hours/week on admin tasks that could be automated
- Ready to invest but skeptical of "AI hype" — want proven results
- Value reliability over flashiness

### Pain Signals (Triggers for Outreach)
- Hiring for admin/ops roles (signal: they're scaling manual work instead of automating)
- Posting about being overwhelmed on social media
- Asking about CRM or automation tools in industry groups
- Website is outdated (>3 years old) with no lead capture
- Using 5+ disconnected tools with no integrations
- Lost a deal because they couldn't follow up fast enough

### Qualification Criteria (BANT)
| Criterion | Must Have | Nice to Have |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **Budget** | $2,500+ available for first engagement | $5,000+/mo for ongoing |
| **Authority** | Decision-maker in conversation | Budget pre-approved |
| **Need** | Specific operational pain they can articulate | Already tried solutions |
| **Timeline** | Ready to start within 30 days | Urgent pain driving timeline |

## Secondary ICP: The Growth-Stage Scaler

### Demographics
- **Business type**: Digital agency, SaaS startup, e-commerce brand
- **Revenue**: $1M-$10M annual
- **Team size**: 10-50 employees
- **Has technical staff**: Yes, but overloaded
- **Decision-maker**: CEO, COO, or VP Operations

### Differentiator from Primary
These prospects have some systems already but are outgrowing them. They need architecture upgrades, not first-time setup. Higher deal values, more complex requirements.

## Anti-ICP (Do NOT Pursue)

| Profile | Why Not | Refer To |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Solopreneurs under $100K revenue | Can't afford our minimum. ROI doesn't justify investment. | DIY resources, Zapier |
| Enterprise (500+ employees) | We can't serve their scale or compliance needs | Enterprise agencies |
| Businesses wanting "just a website" | Commodity service, margins too thin | Freelancers, Squarespace |
| Tire-kickers wanting free advice | Time drain with zero conversion | Blog content, courses |
| Businesses with no budget authority present | Decision loops kill deals | Ask them to bring the decision-maker |

## Channel Priorities

### Tier 1 — Primary Channels (60% of effort)
| Channel | Strategy | Metric |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **LinkedIn** | Thought leadership + direct outreach to ICP | 10 qualified conversations/month |
| **Referrals** | Structured referral program from satisfied clients | 2-3 referrals/month |
| **Local networking** | BNI, Chamber of Commerce, trade associations | 1-2 qualified leads/month |

### Tier 2 — Supporting Channels (30% of effort)
| Channel | Strategy | Metric |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Content marketing** | Blog + case studies showing tangible results | 500 organic visits/month |
| **SEO** | Local SEO + service-line landing pages | Top 3 for "AI automation [city]" |
| **Email nurture** | Weekly value emails to opt-in list | 25% open rate, 3% click rate |

### Tier 3 — Experimental (10% of effort)
| Channel | Strategy | Metric |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **YouTube** | Tutorial content demonstrating capabilities | 100 subscribers first 6 months |
| **Paid ads** | LinkedIn ads targeting ICP job titles | Test with $500/month cap |
| **Partnerships** | White-label for agencies | 1 partner in first quarter |

## Messaging Framework

### Core Message
"We build the AI-powered operating system your business has been missing — so you can stop drowning in operations and start growing."

### Message by Awareness Stage
| Stage | Message | Channel |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Unaware** | "Most SMBs spend 40% of their time on tasks AI could handle in minutes" | Social content, ads |
| **Problem-aware** | "You're not the only business owner working IN the business instead of ON it" | Blog, email |
| **Solution-aware** | "Here's how automation actually works for a business like yours" (case study) | Case studies, webinars |
| **Product-aware** | "Here's exactly what we'd build for you and what it costs" | Proposals, demos |
| **Most aware** | "Ready to start? Here's how onboarding works" | Sales conversation |
