# Platform Specifications Reference

## LinkedIn

### Character Limits
- Post body: 3,000 characters (sweet spot: 1,200-1,500)
- Comment: 1,250 characters
- Headline (profile): 220 characters
- Article: 125,000 characters
- Carousel/Document: PDF format, up to 300 pages (practical: 8-12 slides)

### Image Specs
- Single image: 1200x1200 (square) or 1200x628 (landscape)
- Carousel/Document: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait, preferred)
- Profile photo: 400x400
- Banner: 1584x396
- Max file size: 10MB (images), 100 slides (documents)

### Video Specs
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square) or 16:9 (landscape) or 9:16 (vertical)
- Length: 3 seconds to 10 minutes (sweet spot: 30-90 seconds)
- Max file size: 5GB
- Resolution: 1080p recommended

### Posting Frequency
- Optimal: 3-5 posts per week
- Minimum viable: 2 per week
- Maximum before diminishing returns: 1 per day (weekdays)
- Weekends: Lower reach, skip unless content is time-sensitive

### Best Posting Times (by audience type)
- B2B decision makers: Tuesday-Thursday, 7:00-8:00 AM local time
- General professionals: Tuesday-Thursday, 12:00 PM or 5:00-6:00 PM
- Tech industry: Wednesday, 10:00 AM
- Recruiters/HR: Tuesday, 10:00 AM
- Note: These are starting points. Use LinkedIn analytics to find YOUR audience's active hours.

### Hashtag Strategy
- Quantity: 3-5 hashtags per post (more than 5 reduces reach)
- Placement: End of post or first comment
- Mix: 1-2 broad (>100K followers), 2-3 niche (<10K followers)
- Create a branded hashtag for campaigns

## Twitter/X

### Character Limits
- Tweet: 280 characters (under 100 gets more engagement)
- Twitter Blue/Premium: 25,000 characters (but long tweets rarely perform)
- Bio: 160 characters
- Display name: 50 characters
- Thread: No hard limit (practical: 5-9 tweets)

### Image Specs
- Single image: 1200x675 (16:9) or 1200x1200 (1:1)
- Up to 4 images per tweet
- GIF: Max 15MB, 1280x1080
- Max file size: 5MB per image

### Video Specs
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (landscape) or 1:1 (square) or 9:16 (vertical)
- Length: 0.5 seconds to 2 minutes 20 seconds (Blue: up to 4 hours)
- Max file size: 512MB
- Resolution: 1920x1080

### Posting Frequency
- Minimum: 1 tweet per day + 5-10 replies/engagements
- Optimal: 3-5 original tweets + active replies throughout the day
- Threads: 2-3 per week
- Maximum: There is no real maximum on Twitter. More activity = more visibility.

### Best Posting Times
- General: 8:00-10:00 AM and 6:00-9:00 PM (local time of target audience)
- B2B/Tech: Weekdays, 9:00-11:00 AM EST
- Consumer: Evenings and weekends
- Breaking news/commentary: Real-time (no schedule)
- Note: Twitter is the most time-zone-sensitive platform. Post for YOUR audience's timezone.

### Hashtag Strategy
- Quantity: 0-2 per tweet (more looks spammy on Twitter)
- Trending hashtags: Use only if genuinely relevant to your content
- Thread hashtags: Only in the first tweet, if at all

## Instagram

### Character Limits
- Caption: 2,200 characters (front-load value in first 125 characters before fold)
- Bio: 150 characters
- Comment: 2,200 characters
- Story text: No hard limit but keep it readable (under 100 characters per screen)

### Image Specs
- Feed post (square): 1080x1080
- Feed post (portrait): 1080x1350 (preferred -- takes more screen space)
- Feed post (landscape): 1080x566
- Story/Reel: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Carousel: Up to 10 slides, same dimensions as feed posts
- Profile photo: 320x320

### Video Specs
- Reels: 9:16 vertical, 15-90 seconds (sweet spot: 15-30 seconds)
- Feed video: Up to 60 minutes (sweet spot: under 60 seconds)
- Stories: 15 seconds per segment (auto-splits longer videos)
- Resolution: 1080x1920 for Reels/Stories
- Max file size: 4GB

### Posting Frequency
- Feed posts: 3-5 per week (1 per day max)
- Stories: 3-7 per day (keep audience engaged between feed posts)
- Reels: 3-5 per week (Instagram prioritizes Reels in discovery)
- Minimum viable: 3 feed posts + daily Stories

### Best Posting Times
- General: 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM and 7:00 - 9:00 PM (local)
- B2B: Tuesday-Friday, 11:00 AM
- E-commerce: Wednesday and Friday, 11:00 AM
- Lifestyle/personal: Evenings and weekends
- Note: Instagram Insights shows when YOUR followers are active. Use that data.

### Hashtag Strategy
- Quantity: 20-30 per post (Instagram allows up to 30)
- Mix: 5 large (>500K posts), 10 medium (10K-500K), 10-15 small niche (<10K)
- Placement: End of caption or first comment (no difference in reach)
- Rotate hashtag sets every 2-3 weeks to avoid shadow-ban triggers
- Research hashtags in Explore to verify they're not banned

## TikTok

### Character Limits
- Caption: 2,200 characters
- Bio: 80 characters
- Comment: 150 characters

### Video Specs
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical ONLY (horizontal/square suppressed by algorithm)
- Length: 15 seconds to 10 minutes (sweet spot: 15-30 seconds for new accounts)
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- Max file size: 287MB (mobile), 500MB (desktop)
- Frame rate: 30fps minimum

### Posting Frequency
- Optimal: 1-3 per day
- Minimum: 3-5 per week
- Maximum: 4 per day (more can dilute performance)
- Consistency is critical -- algorithm rewards daily posting

### Best Posting Times
- General: 7:00-9:00 AM, 12:00-3:00 PM, 7:00-11:00 PM (local)
- Gen Z: Later evening, 8:00 PM - 12:00 AM
- Business content: 7:00-9:00 AM (catching morning scroll)
- Note: TikTok analytics shows follower activity times after 100+ followers

### Hashtag Strategy
- Quantity: 3-5 per video
- Always include one broad hashtag related to your niche
- Use 1-2 trending hashtags if genuinely relevant
- Avoid #fyp #foryou #foryoupage -- they do nothing
- Niche-specific hashtags outperform generic ones

## Facebook

### Character Limits
- Post: 63,206 characters (sweet spot: 40-80 characters for engagement)
- Comment: 8,000 characters
- Page description: 255 characters
- Group post: Same as regular post

### Image Specs
- Feed post: 1200x630 (landscape) or 1080x1080 (square)
- Cover photo: 820x312
- Event cover: 1200x628
- Group cover: 1640x856
- Profile photo: 170x170
- Max file size: 30MB

### Video Specs
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (landscape), 1:1 (square), 9:16 (vertical for Reels)
- Length: Up to 240 minutes (sweet spot: 1-3 minutes)
- Resolution: 1080p
- Max file size: 10GB
- Live video: Up to 8 hours

### Posting Frequency
- Pages: 1-2 per day
- Groups: 1-3 per day (community posts, questions, discussions)
- Minimum: 3 per week
- Stories: 1-3 per day

### Best Posting Times
- General: 1:00-4:00 PM weekdays
- B2B: Wednesday, 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- B2C: Thursday-Sunday, 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
- Local business: During business hours + lunch time
- Groups: Evenings and weekends (when members browse casually)

### Hashtag Strategy
- Quantity: 2-5 per post (Facebook hashtags are less impactful than other platforms)
- Use for discoverability in Groups more than feed posts
- Branded hashtags for campaigns
- Don't overuse -- Facebook users perceive heavy hashtag use as spammy
