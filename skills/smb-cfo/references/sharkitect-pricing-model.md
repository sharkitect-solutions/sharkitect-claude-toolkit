# Sharkitect Digital Pricing Model

## Service Lines & Pricing

### Virtual Digital Revamp (VDR)
**What**: Complete digital transformation — website, branding, SEO, automation setup
**Pricing**: $5,000-$15,000 one-time + $500-$2,000/mo ongoing management
**Margin target**: 55-65% gross
**Typical timeline**: 4-8 weeks for initial build, then ongoing
**Cost drivers**: Design tools, hosting, domain, initial setup labor

### Revenue Launch Ramp (RLR)
**What**: Lead generation system — ads, landing pages, CRM setup, nurture sequences
**Pricing**: $3,000-$8,000 setup + $1,000-$3,000/mo management + ad spend (passed through)
**Margin target**: 60-70% gross (excluding ad spend pass-through)
**Typical timeline**: 2-4 weeks setup, 3-month minimum commitment
**Cost drivers**: Ad platform fees, CRM costs, content creation

### Streamline Workflow (SLW)
**What**: Business process automation — n8n workflows, integrations, AI agent setup
**Pricing**: $2,500-$10,000 per workflow system + $500-$1,500/mo maintenance
**Margin target**: 65-75% gross
**Typical timeline**: 1-3 weeks per workflow system
**Cost drivers**: API costs, hosting (n8n), AI API usage (Claude/OpenAI)

### Custom Project Scope (CPS)
**What**: Bespoke projects outside standard service lines
**Pricing**: $150-$175/hr, minimum 10-hour engagement
**Margin target**: 50-60% gross
**Typical timeline**: Varies
**Cost drivers**: Project-dependent

## Pricing Rules

### Non-Negotiables
1. **Minimum engagement**: $2,500 (below this, refer to DIY resources)
2. **Hourly floor**: $150/hr for any custom work
3. **No free work**: Initial consultation is free (30 min max). Everything else is billable.
4. **Scope documentation**: Every project must have a signed scope document before work begins
5. **Change order pricing**: Any scope additions are quoted separately at standard rates

### Bundle Discounts
- 2 service lines: 10% off total
- 3 service lines: 15% off total (requires CEO review)
- Full stack (all 3 core + ongoing): 20% off (requires CEO approval, must maintain 45% minimum margin)

### Payment Terms
- Setup/project work: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
- Monthly retainers: Net 30, auto-pay preferred
- Tier 0/1 enterprise: Net 45 available with CEO approval
- Late payment: 1.5% monthly after 30 days overdue

### Break-Even Analysis
- Fixed costs: ~$400/mo
- Target margin: 55% average
- Break-even revenue: $400 / 0.55 = ~$730/mo
- Current capacity: 8-12 active clients
- Revenue target per client: $1,000-$3,000/mo average

## Pricing Decision Quick Reference

When evaluating a deal, check in order:
1. Is the total value above minimum engagement ($2,500)? No → decline
2. Does the hourly rate equivalent stay above $150? No → renegotiate scope
3. Does gross margin stay above minimum for this service line? No → decline
4. Is the client within our ICP? No → refer out or price at premium
5. Does this require custom development? Yes → add 30% buffer to estimate
