## Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Target | Red Flags (ban these) |
|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| **Confident** (7-9) | Active voice, present tense, declarative. "Here's what we'll build." | "might", "could", "possibly", "potentially", "we believe that", "it's worth noting" |
| **Direct** (7-9) | Lead with the point. 12-18 word sentences. One idea per paragraph. | "in order to", "it's important to understand", "as we discussed", "moving forward", paragraphs >5 sentences |
| **Expert** (6-8) | Precise terms when needed, plain language otherwise. Cite specific outcomes. | "best-in-class", "world-class", "cutting-edge", "leverage" (verb), "synergy", unexplained jargon |
| **Approachable** (6-8) | Smart friend who's technical. "You" > "we". Contractions natural. | "Dear valued client", formal salutations, "please do not hesitate", >1 exclamation mark per piece |
| **Action-Oriented** (7-9) | Every piece ends with clear next step. Specific timelines/deliverables. | "consider doing X" without recommendation, "various options available", summary without recommendation |

## Absolute Bans (never use)

| Banned | Use Instead |
|--------|-------------|
| "Leverage" (as verb) | "Use", "apply", "build on" |
| "Synergy" / "synergize" | Describe the specific benefit |
| "Best-in-class" | Cite specific metrics |
| "Paradigm shift" | Describe what actually changed |
| "Please do not hesitate" | "Let us know" or "reach out anytime" |
| "At the end of the day" | Delete -- sentence works without it |
| "It goes without saying" | Delete the phrase entirely |
| "Think outside the box" | Describe the specific creative approach |
| "Low-hanging fruit" | "Quick wins" or describe specifically |

## Contextual Bans

| Term | Banned When | Allowed When |
|------|------------|--------------|
| "AI-powered" | Marketing without specifics | Technical docs explaining what the AI does |
| "Scalable" | Vague claim | Specific ("scales to 10K concurrent") |
| "Robust" | Vague adjective | Technical context with specific meaning |
| "Seamless" | Always banned | Never -- nothing is actually seamless |
| "Revolutionary" | Almost always | Never -- let the work speak |

## AI Content Traps (check every AI-generated piece)

1. **Over-qualification**: hedge words ("might", "could", "potentially")
2. **Symmetric structures**: parallel sentence patterns (vary rhythm instead)
3. **Filler transitions**: "Furthermore", "Moreover", "In addition" (delete these)
4. **Generic closings**: "I hope this helps!" (end with specific next step)
5. **Enthusiasm inflation**: "That's a great question!" (just answer it)
